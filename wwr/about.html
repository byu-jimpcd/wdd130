Paddle Rush

Our Mission

At Paddle Rush, our mission is to create unforgettable experiences by promoting safety, teamwork and respect for nature. We believe this is a great activity for people to connect with the nature and make lasting memories.

History

Paddle Rush was founded by a group of locals that loved adventures and were passionate about whitewater rafting. This company began as a small local activity among friends and grew into a trustworthy company known for delivering adventures for any type of adventurers

This company, founded in the heart of Peru's rivers, seeks to help visitors discover the beauty of Peru's nature

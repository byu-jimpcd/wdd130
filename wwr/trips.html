Family River Adventure

A safe and exciting rafting experience perfect for families and beginners. This half-day trip includes professional guides, safety equipment, and breathtaking river scenery. Participants will enjoy gentle rapids while learning the basics of whitewater rafting in a fun and welcoming environment.

River Expedition

Spend a full day exploring exciting rapids and beautiful landscapes. This adventure is designed for guests seeking a balance of excitement and nature. Lunch, transportation, and all rafting equipment are included.

Los Dientes del Diablo

Designed for experienced adventurers, Los Dientes del Diablo features powerful rapids, technical river sections, and an unforgettable adrenaline rush. This two-day expedition includes camping, meals, expert guides, and premium safety gear.
